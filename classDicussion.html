Restoration Shaman call upon the element of water and use the healing properties and their totems to keep their allies alive during battle. Below you will find the main rotational spells you will need to use to heal your allies. Restoration Shaman utilize spells such as Riptide, Chain Heal, and Healing Wave to keep their allies healed while also using spells such as Flame Shock, Lightning Bolt, and Lava Burst to assit in damaging enemies. Cooldowns such as Healing Tide Totem, Spirit Link, and Mana Tide Totem can be used every few minutes to assist the group with a bigger impact.

Riptide: Restorative waters wash over a friendly target, healing them for (66.25% of Spell power) and an additional (76% of Spell power) over 15 sec. Flame Shock: Sears the target with fire, causing (35% of Spell power) Fire damage and then an additional (210% of Spell power) Fire damage over 24 sec. Spirit Link Totem: Summons a totem at the target location for 6 sec, which reduces damage taken by all party and raid members within 10 yards by 10%.
