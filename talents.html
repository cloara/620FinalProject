Talent Review for Restoration Shaman for Patch 9.0

Level 15
Torrent Passive: Increases the initial heal from Riptide by 20%.

Undulation Passive: Every third Healing Wave or Healing Surge heals for an additional 50%.

Unleash Life 4% Base Mana - 40yd Range
Instant Cast - 15sec cooldown
Unleashes elemental forces of Life, healing a friendly target for (130% of Spell power) and increasing the effect of the Shaman's next direct heal by 35%.
Level 25
Echo of the Elements Passive: Riptide, Healing Stream Totem, and Lava Burst now have 2 charges. Effects that reset their remaining cooldown will instead grant 1 charge.

Deluge Passive: Healing Wave, Healing Surge, and Chain Heal heal for an additional 20% on targets affected by your Healing Rain or Riptide.

Surge of Earth 10% Base Mana - 40yd Range
Instant Case - 20 sec cooldown
Consumes up to 3 charges of Earth Shield to heal up to 6 allies near your Earth Shield target for [(100% of spell power) / 3 * (1 + Versatility)] per charge consumed.
Level 30
Spirit Wolf Passive: While transformed into a Ghost Wolf, you gain 5% movement speed and 5% damage reduction every 1 sec, stacking up to 4 times.

Earthgrab Totem 2.5 Base Mana - 35yd Range
Instant Cast - 30 sec cooldown
Summons a totem at the target location for 20 sec. The totem pulses every 2 sec, rooting all enemies within 8yds for 8 sec. Enemies previously rooted by the totem will instead suffer 50% movement speed reduction.

Static Charge Passive: Reduces the cooldown of Capacitor Totem by 5 sec for each enemy it stuns, up to a maximum reduction of 20 sec.
Level 35
Ancestral Vigor Passive: Targets you heal with Healing Wave, Healing Surge, Chain Heal, or Riptide's initial heal gain 10% increased health for 10 sec.

Earthern Wall Totem 11% Base Mana - 40yd Range
Instant Cast - 1 min cooldown
Summons a totem with (100 * Total health / 100) health for 15 sec. (35 * spell power / 100) damage from each attack against allies within 10yds of the totem is redirected to the totem.

Ancestral Protection Totem 11% Base Mana - 40yd Range
Instant Cast - 5 min cooldown
Summons a totem at the target location for 30 sec. All allies within 20yds of the totem gain 10% increased health. If an ally dies, the totem will be consumed to allow them to Recincarnate with 20% health and mana. Cannot recincarnate an ally who dies to massive damage.
Level 40
Nature's Guardian Passive: When your health is brought below 35%, you instantly heal for 20% of your maximum health. Cannot occur more than every 45 sec.

Graceful Spirit Passive: Reduces the cooldown of Spiritwalker's Grace by 60 sec and increases your movement speed by 20% while it is active.

Windrush Totem 40yd Range
Instant Cast - 2 min cooldown
Summons a totem at the target location for 15 sec, continually granting all allies who pass within 10yds 60% increased movement speed for 5 sec.
Level 45
Flash Flood Passive: When you consume Tidal Waves, the cast time of your next heal is reduced by 20%.

Downpour 15% Base Mana - 40yd Range
1.5 sec cast - 5 sec cooldown
A burst of water at the target location heals up to six injured allies within 12yds for (175% of spell power). Cooldown increased by 5 sec for each target effectively healed.

Cloudburst Totem 8.6% Base Mana
Instant Case - 30 sec cooldown
Summons a totem at your feet for 15 sec that collects power from all healing spells. When the totem expires or dies, the stored power is released, healing all injured allies with 40yds for 30% for all healing down while it was active, divided evenly among targets. Casting this spell a second time recalls the totem and release the healing.
Level 50
High Tide Passive: Every 10000 mana you spend brings a High Tide, making your next 2 Chain Heals heal for an addional 10% and not reduce with each jump.

Wellspring 20% Base Mana - 30yd Range
1.5 sec cast - 20 sec cooldown
Creates a surge of water that flows forward, healing friendly targets in a wide arc in front of you for (190% of spell power).

Ascendance Instant Cast - 3 min cooldown
Transform into a water Ascendant, duplicating all healing you deal for 15 sec and immediately healing for (876% of spell power). Ascendant healing is distributed evenly among allies with 20yds.
